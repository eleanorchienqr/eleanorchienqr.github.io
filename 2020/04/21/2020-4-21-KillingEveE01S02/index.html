美剧《杀死伊芙》台词精读

Posted on 2020-04-21 Edited on 2021-03-11 In Entertainment Views: Valine:
Symbols count in article: 2.5k Reading time ≈ 2 mins.

本篇文章是《杀死伊芙》系列剧集片段的台词精读，下线节奏抓人眼球。

Season01Episode01

Sentences

  1. Why are you so perky? 你怎么这么精神？

  2. From what I can eavesdrop on, a Russian sex-trafficking politician's been murdered in Vienna. 从我能偷听到的来看，一个参与性贩卖的俄罗斯政客在维也纳被谋杀了。

  3. I know, bum-out. 很烦人吧。

  4. Was it a contract kill? 是雇凶杀人吗？

  5. I'd nail a cousin to work with that woman. 为了跟她一起工作我可以掐死一个表弟。

  6. I'll put in a good word. 我会帮你好好说话的。

  7. We know it's a tight turnaround. 我们也知道时间很紧。

  8. All that from a tiny punture. 全是一个小伤口造成的。

  9. I'd paralyze you with saxitoxin and suffocate you in your sleep. 我会用蛤蚌毒素麻痹你，然后睡觉的时候把你闷死。

  10. Chop you into the smallest bits I could manage, boil you down, put you in a blender, then take you to work in a flask, and flush you down in a restaurant toilet. 切成小块，水煮搅拌，放在小瓶子里带去工作，然后在餐厅洗手间冲走。

  11. She's prolific. 多次作案。

  12. I've compiling these cases for months. 我已经搜集资料几个月了。

  13. I'm sorry if your husband is boring you, too, but that doesn't give you an excuse to go rogue at work. 找借口在工作中耍无赖。

  14. I'm with the security team monitoring Kasia Molkovska. 我是负责监护卡莎的保安小队的。

  15. You'll sound like a nutter. 你会听起来像个疯子。

  16. On top of that, you failed at your actual job. 最重要的是，你没有做好本职工作。

  17. Clearly there's going to be some reshuffling in your department. 显然你的部门需要人事调整了。

  18. Your things will be biked to you. 你们的东西会送到你们手中。

  19. She's highly skilled, as yet untraceable. 技术高超难以追踪。

      • the bird life is fabulously prolific here 这个地区的鸟类异常繁多
